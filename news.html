Vint Surf Was Impressed By Ethiopia's Preparation For The International Conference Of Internet Governance.

Vint Surf was impressed by Ethiopia's preparation for the International Conference of Internet Governance.

The Minister of Innovation and Technology has discussed Vant Surf, known as the internet's father, Best Mollah (PHD). Vint Surf said he was impressed by the way Ethiopia hosted the International Conference of Internet Management and was pleased with it. He said Ethiopia has demonstrated its commitment to internet management by closing the conference in Addis Ababa. He also said he had seen the Ethiopian hospitality at the conference. The Minister of Innovation and Technology, Best Mollah (PHD), Vant Surf, came to Ethiopia and thanked them for participating in the conference and sharing their experience with the International Network Management Conference. They worked together on issues that could be addressed..
