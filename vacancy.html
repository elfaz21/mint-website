Vacancy announcement for Technical Support and data collector.
Easy of Doing Business Project Expansion
Technical Support Staff Job Requirement Description.
Data Collector Job Vacancy - Ministry of Innovation and Technology, Digital Transformation Program.
Location: Addis Ababa, Bahir Dar, and Dire Dawa
Career Level: Entry-level
Employment Type: Contract, 1 year.
Salary: Attractive
Job Description Currently, Ministry of Innovation and Technology, Digital Transformation Program is seeking to hire Technical Support team and PTC (business process, time, and cost) data collectors for the Easy of Doing Business project, which is under execution in Addis Ababa, Bahir Dar, Adama and Dire Dawa City administrations. The candidate is mainly expected to collect PTC data for selected government organizations services provided for the public, business process workflow/procedure, its time, and corresponding service costs with high level of quality, discipline, and commitment, in a short time.
Major Duties and Responsibilities:
Understand land registration and construction permit offices business processes.
Collect PTC data, identify any business process improvement areas,
Review collected PTC related data validation.
Configure business process workflow based on the collected PTC data.
Assist and collaborate with the respective office’s IT and business owner team throughout the project life cycle.
Study existing office work environment system and network infrastructure
Existing customer service provision gap analysis
Communicate findings with respective project stakeholders and report for the project manager.
Understand the national business portal platform working principles and workflow configuration technique.
Website development, maintenance using open-source technologies.
Data entry on content management portal.
Any internal business process automation works related initiative support. Identification of back-office applications integration requirements if any and reporting to project manager.
Conduct customer satisfaction survey on the platform’s usability.
Conduct caseworker’s satisfaction survey, compile the result and report to project manager if any change is required.
Conduct customer awareness campaigns, collaborate with local IT service centers for the expansion of online services provision.
Conduct Caseworkers capacity building IT trainings and awareness creation sessions.
work hand-in-hand with project manager, IT service centers, project consultant, regional IT offices, awareness and training coordinators etc.
Receive feedback from the head office (from the project manager, business portal super admin) and make a correction or modification accordingly.
Daily Monitor the business portal health status, immediately resolve issues and escalate major issues.
Perform any relevant duties and responsibilities assigned by the immediate supervisor /s.
Job Requirement
Required Qualifications:
BSc in Computer Science, Information Technology, Information Science, Management Information System and related fields
Experience:
Experience of Business requirement elicitation.
Experience of statistical data collection.
Technical and Other Skills
knowledge and skill with data collection procedure (questioners, interviews, tests etc.)
Knowledge of using surveying tools including Microsoft office tools
A working knowledge of Amharic and English language is mandatory.
Able to work accurately.
Able to work independently.
Have good communication skills.
Have a good presentation skill
Able to work in high pressure
Able and willing to relocate to Addis Ababa, Bahir Dar, Adama or Dire Dawa
Send your CV and credentials to the mail address MintHR@mint.gov.et
